Trust & security

Your keys and funds stay yours.

TradeForge is a local desktop application. It signs transactions with your own wallet through your chosen Solana RPC and never holds, custodies, or transfers your funds.

Self-custody, always
TradeForge only submits swaps you configure. Your funds stay in your own Solana wallet — the app never has custody.
